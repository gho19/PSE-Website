Who Are We?


Pi Sigma Epsilon, Eta Iota chapter at the University of Michigan is a co-ed Professional Business Fraternity structured on four primary pillars: Excellence, Equity, Community, and Passion. Yet beyond this, we are a diverse body of undergraduates constantly working to better ourselves, each other, and the world around us.

PSE is composed of 100+ members hailing from a variety of different hometowns, majors, and career paths unified under the shared interests of professional growth and brotherly love. Together we harmonize these two ambitions within various endeavors; we build upon our business acumen, skills and networks through a comprehensive professional development semester and real-world consulting experience with local companies while cultivating lifelong friendships thanks to memorable social events, philanthropic fundraisers, and assorted bonding activities. We pride ourselves on our commitment to ourselves and each other to ensure that we do not just succeed as young professionals, but as scholars, community members, and people, too.



Kate’s President Update





Hi everyone! On behalf of our entire fraternity, I would like to welcome you to the official website of Pi Sigma Epsilon’s Eta Iota Chapter at the University of Michigan.


PSE is by far my most favorite community that I have joined at Michigan. When I joined two semesters ago, I had very limited knowledge on professionalism in the business world. Soon after I joined, PSE was able to provide me with guidance both in figuring out what careers I want to further explore and how to develop professionally. In addition, my network has expanded immensely. I now know so many more familiar faces and have found upperclassmen mentors that I know I can rely on both professionally and socially. PSE is a community that allows me to feel prepared for my future due to the diverse group of members with varying backgrounds and majors. There are so many opportunities to become involved right when you join the club. Not only have I grown professionally, but I have also found some of my closest friends through this fraternity through our variety of social events.


By joining PSE you are committing to developing your knowledge of the business world, while enhancing your business acumen. However, most importantly, you are committing to join a brotherhood that reflects a bond unlike any other on campus. A family


Continue to check out our website to learn more about our experiences and involvements, and most importantly, APPLY TODAY! If you have any questions at all, please feel free to reach out to me at krassin@umich.edu. Come find your PSEople!

Kate Rassin – President, PSE
