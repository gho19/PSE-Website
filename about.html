THE MISSION OF PI SIGMA EPSILON IS TO DEVELOP THE SALES AND MARKETING SKILLS OF ITS MEMBERS THROUGH LIFELONG OPPORTUNITIES.

Who Are We?


Pi Sigma Epsilon is a co-ed Professional Business Fraternity structured on four primary pillars: Excellence, Equity, Community, and Passion. The Pi Sigma Epsilon, Eta Iota chapter at the University of Michigan was founded in 2015 and has since grown to include over 90 active members, while still growing. Our mission is to develop the general business and professional skills of our members through experiential learning and professional development. This includes an ambitious new member term, participation in consulting projects with local and national companies, and the opportunity for positions on the Executive and Auxiliary Boards. We value diversity and therefore are open to all majors.
